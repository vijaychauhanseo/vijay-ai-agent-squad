## Core Concept
SEO competitor analysis is the process of identifying your search competitors, understanding what makes them rank, and finding opportunities to outrank them. It's the foundation of any data-driven SEO strategy.

## Key Takeaways
- SEO competitors ≠ business competitors. Sites competing for your keywords are your real SEO rivals
- Start by identifying who ranks for your 5–10 most important keywords — those are your true SEO competitors
- Keyword gap analysis: find keywords competitors rank for that you don't (low-hanging fruit)
- Backlink gap analysis: find sites linking to competitors but not you (outreach targets)
- Content gap: identify topics competitors cover comprehensively that you haven't touched
- Don't copy competitors — understand WHY they rank, then do it better
- Run competitor analysis when: launching a new site, planning content calendar, noticing ranking drops

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **4-step competitor analysis process**: Identify → Analyze keywords → Analyze backlinks → Analyze content
- **Keyword gap tool**: Ahrefs → Competitive Research → Keyword Gap → enter up to 5 competitors
- **Backlink gap tool**: Ahrefs → Competitive Research → Link Intersect → find prospects linking to competitors
- **Content gap**: Find top competitor pages by organic traffic → identify which topics you're missing
