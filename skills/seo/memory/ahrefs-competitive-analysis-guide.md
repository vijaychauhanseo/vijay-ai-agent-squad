## Core Concept
A lean 6-step framework for competitive analysis that covers share of voice, pricing/features, user reviews, and purchasing convenience. Goes beyond SEO into full market positioning analysis.

## Key Takeaways
- Competitive analysis = understanding where you win and lose vs. competitors across all dimensions
- Share of voice (SOV) = what % of total market visibility you own across search, social, and AI
- Don't just analyze rankings — analyze product, pricing, reviews, and buying friction too
- User reviews on G2/Capterra/Trustpilot reveal exactly what customers hate about competitors (content opportunity)
- Competitive analysis should inform: content strategy, product positioning, pricing, and messaging

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
**6-Step Lean Competitive Analysis**:
1. **Identify direct competitors** — who competes for same customers + same keywords
2. **Compare share of voice** — organic search + social + AI mentions (use Ahrefs + Brand Radar)
3. **Compare pricing and features** — where do you over/under-deliver vs. competitors?
4. **Find strong/weak points from user reviews** — G2, Trustpilot, Reddit, YouTube comments
5. **Compare purchasing convenience** — UX, checkout flow, onboarding friction
6. **Present conclusions** — actionable recommendations, not just observations
