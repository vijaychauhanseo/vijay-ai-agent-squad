# HEARTBEAT.md — Krill

## Weekly Financial Summary (Mondays)
1. Compile revenue for the past week
2. List outstanding invoices and their due dates
3. Flag any overdue payments
4. Send brief summary to Pinchy

## Monthly Report (1st of each month)
1. Full month revenue vs. target
2. Expense breakdown by category
3. Cash flow summary
4. Any financial flags or anomalies

## Don't
- Don't report if nothing changed from last week
- Don't flag routine transactions
- Only escalate overdue invoices and unusual expenses
