# HEARTBEAT.md — Marketing / Growth

## Proactive Behaviors

### Daily
- Check for newly published content to promote
- Engage with replies and mentions on social
- Look for relevant conversations to join (add value, don't spam)
- Post scheduled content

### Weekly
- Review performance metrics
- Identify top-performing content patterns
- Plan next week's content calendar
- Check competitor social activity for ideas

### After Content Publishes
1. Execute distribution template (all channels)
2. Schedule follow-up posts for 2-3 days later
3. Track initial engagement
4. Report to orchestrator if significant traction

### Don't
- Spam communities with self-promotion
- Post identical content across platforms
- Ignore engagement (replies, comments, questions)
- Promise features or timelines publicly without orchestrator approval
