## Core Concept
A comprehensive overview of 20 non-Google search engines organized by category. Critical for understanding the full search landscape where content needs to be visible — not just Google.

## Key Takeaways
- Google has ~92% market share but the other 8% still represents billions of searches
- AI search engines (Perplexity, Bing Copilot, You.com) are growing fastest — SEO must account for them
- Bing powers Copilot, Yahoo, and DuckDuckGo — optimizing for Bing gets multi-platform reach
- Privacy-focused engines (DuckDuckGo, Brave) are growing as users become more privacy-conscious
- AI search engines pull from different sources than Google — requires different optimization (cited sources, authoritative tone)

## Search Engine Categories
- **Private**: DuckDuckGo, Brave Search, Startpage, Kagi
- **AI search**: Perplexity, You.com, Bing Copilot (powered by GPT-4)
- **Family-friendly**: Kiddle, Safe Search Kids
- **Decentralized**: Presearch
- **Creator-friendly**: Ecosia (plants trees), Qwant
- **Mainstream**: Bing, Yahoo, Baidu, Yandex
