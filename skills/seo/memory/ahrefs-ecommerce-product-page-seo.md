## Core Concept
14 actionable tactics for optimizing ecommerce product pages for search — covering keyword research, content, technical SEO, structured data, and indexing strategy. Highly applicable to any page selling a product or service.

## Key Takeaways
- Product page SEO starts with keyword research — align product names (H1s) to actual search terms
- Unique, helpful content differentiates product pages from competitors (manufacturers' descriptions hurt rankings)
- Structured data (Product schema + Merchant Center) unlocks rich results in Google Shopping
- Customer reviews are both trust signals and unique content that Google values
- Internal linking from blog posts to product pages transfers authority and drives conversions
- Variant management (color/size options) must be handled carefully to avoid duplicate content
- Out-of-stock and discontinued products need a strategy — don't just 404 them

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
**14-point product page SEO checklist**:
1. Deep keyword research for product names
2. Adjust H1/title tags to match search queries
3. Unique product descriptions (not manufacturer copy)
4. Semantic heading structure (H1 → H2 → H3)
5. Image optimization (descriptive filenames, alt text, compression)
6. Semantic HTML markup
7. Unique meta descriptions per product
8. Product schema + Google Merchant Center integration
9. Customer reviews (UGC content)
10. Internal links from relevant blog content
11. Variant URL management (canonical tags or parameters)
12. XML sitemap inclusion
13. Out-of-stock strategy (301 redirect or keep + update)
14. Selective indexing (noindex thin/duplicate pages)
