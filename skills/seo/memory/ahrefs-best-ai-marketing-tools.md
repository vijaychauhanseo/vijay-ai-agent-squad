## Core Concept
A curated list of 14 AI marketing tools across different marketing functions — from coding/automation to content optimization, brand monitoring, and research. Highly relevant for @learnaiwithvijay content.

## Key Takeaways
- AI is now used across every marketing function: research, creation, optimization, monitoring, automation
- Best tools are specialized, not generalist — pick the right tool for each job
- Ahrefs positions their own AI tools (Brand Radar, AI Content Helper) alongside third-party tools
- AI share of voice (new metric) = how often your brand appears in AI-generated answers

## Tools Covered (14 tools)
1. **ChatGPT** — coding and workflow automation
2. **Ahrefs Brand Radar** — AI share of voice analysis (how often you appear in AI answers)
3. **NapkinAI** — converts text into presentation visuals automatically
4. **Ahrefs AI Content Helper** — on-page SEO optimization suggestions
5. **Grammarly** — quick copy edits and tone adjustment
6. **Browse AI** — no-code web scraping automation
7. **Jasper** — replicating brand/author tone-of-voice at scale
8. **NotebookLM** — grounding AI in your own documents for strategic feedback
9. **Canva** — AI background removal and image editing
10. **Gumloop** — AI-powered brand mention listening
11. **Slack AI** — integrated workflow and knowledge management
12. **Promptless** — auto-updating product documentation
13. **Cloudflare AI audit** — managing which AI crawlers can access your site
14. **Typeform AI** — AI-powered research surveys

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **AI share of voice**: Track how often your brand/content is cited in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini)
- **AI crawler management**: Use Cloudflare to control which AI bots index your content
