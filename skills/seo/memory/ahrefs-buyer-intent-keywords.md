## Core Concept
Buyer intent keywords are search terms that signal a user is ready to make a purchase or take action in the near future. They convert significantly better than informational keywords because the searcher is already in decision-making mode.

## Key Takeaways
- Buyer intent keywords = higher conversion rate, lower traffic volume but higher ROI
- Four types: informational → navigational → commercial investigation → transactional (funnel stages)
- Commercial investigation keywords ("best X", "X vs Y", "X review") are the sweet spot — high intent, still SEO-rankable
- Transactional keywords ("buy X", "X price", "X discount") are best for PPC, not always SEO
- Use Ahrefs Keywords Explorer to filter by keyword intent
- Study competitors' top-converting pages to find their buyer intent keyword clusters
- Match keyword intent to content format: transactional → product page, commercial investigation → comparison/review post

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **The 4-stage intent funnel**: Informational → Navigational → Commercial Investigation → Transactional
- **Buyer keyword patterns**: "best [product]", "[product] review", "[product] vs [product]", "[product] pricing", "buy [product]", "[product] discount", "hire [service]"
- **Finding competitors' buyer keywords**: Ahrefs Site Explorer → Top Pages → filter by low Traffic Value (means high-converting keywords)
