## Core Concept
A 9-step process for writing blog posts that rank AND engage readers — covering topic selection through promotion. Combines SEO fundamentals with content quality principles.

## Key Takeaways
- Start with a proven topic — use Ahrefs to find what people actually search for, don't guess
- Angle matters as much as topic — same topic, different angle = different audience and ranking opportunity
- Outline first, write second — structure prevents rambling and ensures all subtopics are covered
- Headlines are written AFTER the draft, not before — you can't write a great headline until you know what you wrote
- On-page SEO is a quick 30-minute task after writing, not the whole focus
- Promotion is non-negotiable — publishing without promoting = wasted work
- "First draft is just to get words down" — edit ruthlessly in passes

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
**9-Step Blog Writing Process**:
1. **Find proven topic** — keyword research in Ahrefs, look for search volume + low KD
2. **Decide angle** — unique perspective, data-driven, contrarian, beginner-friendly, etc.
3. **Create outline** — H2/H3 structure based on what top-ranking posts cover
4. **Write first draft** — write fast, don't edit while writing
5. **Polish and edit** — cut fluff, improve clarity, add examples
6. **Write headline** — use power words, numbers, brackets for CTR
7. **On-page SEO** — title tag, meta description, image alt text, internal links
8. **Publish** — format properly, add featured image, set correct categories
9. **Promote** — email list, social media, relevant communities, outreach to mentioned people
